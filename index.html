Adventurous Aruki is a kind-hearted and empathetic beast tamer. He adapts well to animals' characteristics and personality, thus, allowing him to tame wild beasts and creatures quickly. Chidori

CV:

Voice Actor

Illust:

Like a wolf, Chidori prowls silently in the woods, observing her pray. Her steps are quieter than snowfall - she patiently lurks in the shadows and waits for the right moment to strike without mercy. Seeming aloof and a bit socially awkward, she actually cares about her comrades very much and will put their lives before her own.

"Without a sound."
